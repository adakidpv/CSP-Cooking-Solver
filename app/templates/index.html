Problem Statement

Plan to cook a large meal on a K-burner stove. You have to cook N dishes, each of which must be started in a clean pan and needs to cook for a certain (uninterrupted) amount of time. You have a limited number of pans. If you need to reuse a pan, it will take a fixed amount of time (3 minutes) to clean it.

Constraints

  • All pans are clean at the start of the process.
  • Each dish requires uninterrupted cooking time.
  • Cleaning time for pans is fixed at 3 minutes.
  • Burners and pans are shared resources.
